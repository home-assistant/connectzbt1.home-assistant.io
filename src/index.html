Home Assistant SkyConnect is the easiest way to add Zigbee support to your Home Assistant instance and make it Matter-ready. It features a transmit power of up to 20 dBm and over-the-air updates. A future firmware update will bring Thread support; allowing SkyConnect to power your Matter and Zigbee networks at the same time.
